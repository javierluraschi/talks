We want Data Science to be reproducible

But data is often strangely downloaded

Use 'pins' to download data

              pins::pin("http://source/wine.csv") %>%
    read.csv()

And share it with your friends

            library(pins)
pin_get("wine", board = "local") %>%
  pin(board = "s3") %>%
  pin(board = "rsconnect")
